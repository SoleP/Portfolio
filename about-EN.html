About me

Hello I’m Sole, and I am an Industrial designer who loves to accept challenges every day. I enjoy all the steps of my profession, from sketching, to 2D/3D computer modeling, to prototyping, etc. I believe that the beauty of it all is to see an idea come to life, materialize. I have been working in this industry for over six years, and it never ceases to amaze me. In this portfolio you’ll find a vast example of my work: simple sketches, complex prototypes and models. Thank you for visiting!
