    • Client: Vertigo
    • Project date: 2018

    Cleaning system for glass curtain walls buildings

    Cleaning system for glass curtain walls buildings. Its design is based on the functionality of two rollers: upper and lower. The first is the largest and is in charge of the first cleaning. This roller is wide and with less “hair”. On the other hand, the upper roller is in charge of fine cleaning and drying, since it rotates at twice the speed of the first. Together with the rollers, it has a sprinkler system that spray the glass with filtered water. This design was Patent in the IMPI and Its participated in “Argentina Innovar 2018”.
