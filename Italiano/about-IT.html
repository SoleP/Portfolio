    A little of me

    I was born in Argentina, where I studied Industrial design at Buenos Aires University. I love to travel and get to know other cultures. So in 2020, I decided to adventure myself and moved to Europe. Since then I have been studying online UX/UX and Web Development. I enjoy learning new stuff, especially related to technology and computers I like accepting challenges every day. I enjoy all the steps of my profession, from sketching, to 2D/3D computer modeling, to prototyping, etc. I believe that the beauty of it all is to see an idea come to life, materialize. I have been working in this industry for over six years, and it never ceases to amaze me. In this portfolio, you’ll find a vast example of my work: simple sketches, complex prototypes, and models.
